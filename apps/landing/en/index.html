About AIVITA

AIVITA is a health technology company based in Tashkent, Uzbekistan. We build AI-powered health tools for individuals and medical institutions across Central Asia. Our platform serves patients, doctors, and clinics in all 14 regions of Uzbekistan, with interfaces in Russian, Uzbek (Latin), and English.

AIVITA's mission is to make quality healthcare accessible to every resident of Uzbekistan — regardless of region, language, or income level.
